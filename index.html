Frequently asked questions

Do I need an appointment?

No — walk-ins are always welcome. If you'd rather have a seat ready when you arrive, you're welcome to call ahead too.

What payment methods do you accept?

Tap, debit, and credit cards are all accepted, alongside standard payment methods.

Is the salon wheelchair accessible?

Yes — the salon is wheelchair accessible, with parking and a restroom on-site.

Do you work with curly or textured hair?

Yes — cutting and styling for curl patterns is one of the salon's specialties, whether you want it diffused, scrunched, or defined.

Where do I go for nails or waxing?

The nail & waxing studio has its own separate entrance around the corner on E 13th Street.

What are your hours?

Open daily, 9:30 AM – 7:00 PM Eastern Time. See the live open/closed status up top, or the full hours table below.
